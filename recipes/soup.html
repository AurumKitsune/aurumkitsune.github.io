Vegatable Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken breast
  • 24oz wishbone italian dressing
  • 3 chopped russet potatoes
  • 1 sliced and halved zucchini
  • 1 sliced and halved squash
  • 2 sliced red bell peppers

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken in just enough oil to coat the bottom
  2. After cooked, add the italian dressing and vegatables
  3. Leave cooking on medium-low heat for and hour, stirring occasionally

Meatloaf Recipe

Ingredients

Meatloaf
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2/3 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 lbs beef
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup parsley
Glaze
  • 3/4 cup ketchup
  • 3/4 cup worchestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. In a sauce pan, put a little bit of oil and cook the onion until soft
  3. While letting the onions cool, combine the ingredients for the glaze
  4. Combine the ingredients for the meatloaf and mix in a third of your glaze
  5. Make 2 meatloaves and put in the oven for 15 minutes
  6. Take the meatloaves out, glaze them with another third of the glaze and put them back in the oven for another 20 minutes
  7. Take the meatloaves out, and glaze with the remaining bit and throw back in the oven for 25 minutes

Tandoori Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken legs
  • 2 cups greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp garam masala

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl
  2. Cover the bowl and leave it in the fridge for 4-8 hours
  3. Put the chicken on a baking sheet with a rack
  4. Cook under the broiler for 5-10 minutes
  5. Flip the chicken and put it back under the broiler for 5-10 minutes
  6. Set oven to 350 degrees and cook for 15 minutes

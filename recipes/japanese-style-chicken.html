Japanese-style Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4-8 chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a wok
  2. Cook on medium for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally

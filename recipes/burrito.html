Burrito Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 cup rice

Instructions

  1. Brown beef in a pan with the onion
  2. Add the tomatoes, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic, and beef broth
  3. Let cook for 3 minutes then add the rice and oregano

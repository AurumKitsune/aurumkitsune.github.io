Tikka Masala Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs tandoori chicken
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 28 oz crushed tomato
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 2 tsp garlic
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Tear and chop the tandoori chicken into small pieces
  2. Cook onion in a pot with a bit of oil until soft
  3. Add tomato paste, paprika, cayenne pepper, garam masala, ginger, garlic to the pot
  4. After a minute add in the rest of the rest of the ingredients
  5. Cook for 15 minutes

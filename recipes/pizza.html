Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

Dough

  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 5 tbsp whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2/3 tsp yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar

Sauce

  • 15 oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/3 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Mix together all the dough ingredients
  2. Knead dough or use a stand mixer with a dough hook
  3. Split into 2 balls and proof for a minimum of 8 hours
  4. Stretch a dough ball out into a 14" circle
  5. Brush some oil on the top of the dough
  6. Mix the ingredients for the sauce
  7. Spread some sauce on the dough
  8. Add grated low-moisture mozzarella on top of the pizza
  9. Bake in oven at 500 degrees for 10-12 minutes

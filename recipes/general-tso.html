General Tso's Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

Tempura Chicken

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 egg

General Tso's Sauce

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 tbsp white wine
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1-2 tsp corn starch

Instructions

Tempura Chicken

  1. Whisk together the egg, flour, and water
  2. Chill the batter with ice
  3. Cut chicken into 1 inch pieces
  4. Coat the chicken pieces in the tempura batter
  5. Fry chicken in hot oil until lightly golden

General Tso's Sauce

  1. Heat a wok/pan to medium heat
  2. Combine soy sauce, garlic, ginger, white wine, rice vinegar, red pepper flakes in the wok/pan
  3. Cook for 2-3 minutes
  4. Add chicken broth and sugar
  5. Combine corn starch with a bit of water
  6. Add corn starch mixture until desired thickness

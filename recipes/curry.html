Indian Curry recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken breast
  • 1 sweet onion
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 1/2 cups rice

Instructions

  1. Chop the onion and cook it in a small bit of oil on medium-low heat until soft
  2. Add the chicken and turn up the heat to medium-high until cooked
  3. Add the ginger and garlic and let toast for one minute
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for another minute
  5. Add the turmeric, cumin, paprika, cayenne power, chicken broth, and heavy cream and cook for 10 minutes on medium-low heat
  6. Boil water and make the rice while waiting
